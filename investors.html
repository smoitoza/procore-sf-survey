The Problem

A $277B market stuck in the past

Corporate real estate teams make million-dollar decisions using tools from the fax machine era.

100+ Page Static PDFs

Every market, every broker, same output: a massive static PDF. No map. No filtering. No way to build a tour list. Corporate teams waste days manually sifting through pages of listings, copying data into spreadsheets just to compare three buildings.

Brokers Don't Speak CFO

Brokers care about the deal, not your financial reports. They send an asking rate and a nice rendering. They never send cash flow schedules, straight-line P&L, or GAAP-format financials. Your finance team rebuilds everything from scratch for every deal.
